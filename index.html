How Block Blast Works

Block Blast is a puzzle game where players place different block shapes onto a grid. When a row or column is completely filled, it clears and creates more space for future moves. The challenge comes from planning ahead and managing the board efficiently.

Beginner Tips

Keep the center of the board as open as possible. Avoid creating isolated gaps that are difficult to fill. Think about the next few pieces instead of only the current move. Save flexible spaces for larger block shapes.

Common Mistakes

Filling the corners too early. Ignoring long-term board management. Using large pieces before checking future placement options. Clearing rows without considering the new board shape.
