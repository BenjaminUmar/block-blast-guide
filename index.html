What is Block Blast?

Block Blast is a puzzle game built around placing differently shaped blocks on a grid. Rows and columns clear when filled completely, rewarding careful planning rather than quick reactions.

Beginner Tips

  • Keep the center open.
  • Plan several moves ahead.
  • Avoid isolated gaps.
  • Save flexible spaces for larger pieces.

Platform Overview

The game is commonly played on Android and iOS devices, and some players also enjoy it on desktop through compatible methods.
